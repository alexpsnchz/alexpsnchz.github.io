About me

Welcome to my personal web portfolio! I'm Álex Pérez-Sánchez, a chemist who graduated from Universitat Autònoma de Barcelona. Within the esteemed Physical Chemistry Unit, I am an active member of the MolBioMed Research Group, led by Dr. Josep M. Lluch and Dr. Àngels González-Lafont.

The MolBioMed Research Group is dedicated to exploring the intersection of chemistry, biology, and medicine. As a member of this dynamic team, I have the privilege of working alongside brilliant minds who share a common goal of unraveling the mysteries of biological systems and developing innovative solutions.

In particular, our research focuses on computational studies of inflammation-related systems, with a special emphasis on enzymes such as cyclooxygenases (COX), lipoxygenases (LOX), and related receptors. By leveraging advanced computational methods, we aim to understand the reactivity and mechanisms underlying these enzymes' activities.

Our collaborative efforts extend beyond fundamental investigations. We aspire to design novel drugs, including photoswitches, which hold the potential to modulate the activity of these enzymes and pave the way for groundbreaking therapies. Our ultimate aim is to contribute to biomedicine by developing innovative strategies for addressing complex challenges, including diseases like cancer.

Through this web portfolio, I invite you to explore the remarkable research conducted within the MolBioMed Research Group. Join me as we delve into the intricacies of biological systems, examine the fascinating world of inflammation-related research, and discover the potential for transformative advancements in medicine.

Thank you for visiting, and I am excited to share our collective passion, research insights, and discoveries with you
